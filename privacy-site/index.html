Puffy & Citrus — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Puffy & Citrus ("the app", "we", "us") is a cozy virtual-pet game. We care about your privacy and built the app to collect as little information as possible. This policy explains what we do and don't collect, and the choices you have.

The short version: The game works fully offline and stores your progress on your own device. We only ever store anything on our servers if you choose to create an optional account for cloud sync. We do not show ads, we do not use analytics or tracking, and we never sell your data.

1. Information stored on your device

By default, all of your game data — your pets, progress, coins, items, and settings — is saved locally on your device only. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

2. Optional account & cloud sync

If you choose to create an account inside the app (so your progress can sync across devices and you can add friends), we store the following through our backend provider, Supabase:

  • A username you pick, and a login identifier derived from it (in the form username@puffycitrus.app).
  • A password, which is stored securely (salted and hashed) by our authentication provider — we never see or store your raw password.
  • Your game save data (the same progress described above), so it can sync.
  • A public display name and basic profile snapshot, used only to enable the optional Friends and gifting features.

That's all. Creating an account is entirely optional — you can play the whole game without one by choosing "play offline".

3. Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, precise or coarse location, contacts, photos, microphone or camera data, or advertising identifiers. The app requests no device permissions beyond internet access (used only for optional cloud sync).

4. Advertising

The current version of Puffy & Citrus contains no advertising and no advertising SDKs. If we introduce advertising in a future version, we will update this policy, disclose it clearly in the app stores, and request any consent required by law before any ad network collects data.

5. Children

Puffy & Citrus is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond the optional username and game save described above. If you are a parent or guardian and would like us to delete an account created by your child, please contact us using the details below and we will remove it promptly.

6. Data retention & deletion

Local data remains until you delete the app or reset progress within the app. If you created a cloud account, you may request deletion of your account and all associated server data at any time by emailing us at the address below; we will delete it within 30 days.

7. Third-party services

We use Supabase to provide optional account and cloud-sync functionality, and the web version is hosted on GitHub Pages. These providers process data only as needed to deliver those services.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect any data stored on our behalf, including encrypted transport (HTTPS) and secured database access. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, noted within the app.

10. Contact

Questions, or want your data deleted? Email us at miniron3@gmail.com.
